Welcome to the Okewunmi Dynasty

The Okewunmi Dynasty is a family rooted in truth, faith, sacrifice, and legacy. At the heart of this great family is Elder Solomon Okewunmi, a man widely respected for his integrity, wisdom, and selfless devotion. Born and raised in Ogun State, Egba, Abeokuta—Itori Oke, Elder Solomon exemplifies a life of service to both family and community.

Now at the age of 71 (in 2025), Elder Solomon continues to stand as a pillar of strength and guidance. By his side is his beloved wife, Deaconess Adebola Okewunmi, a virtuous woman and nurturing mother. Together, they have built a strong family rooted in love, discipline, and godliness.

The Okewunmi Children

Oshode Damilola (née Mama Oshode)

The firstborn of the family, Damilola, stands as a model of resilience, wisdom, and industriousness. A graduate of Computer Science from Ire Ekiti Polytechnic, she is a leather materials mogul. Married to Mr. Oshode Damilare, blessed with two children, Ademide and Iremide.

Engr. Okewunmi Ayomide

The second-born and first son, Ayomide, is a skilled engineer specializing in heavy-duty machine repairs. He is launching “Okewunmi Autos.” Married to Simisola Okewunmi, blessed with a son, Simon Okewunmi.

Okewunmi Oluwagbenga

The third-born, Oluwagbenga, embodies entrepreneurial creativity in the shoe and fashion industry. Married and building a family on love, respect, and godly values.

Okewunmi Opeyemi (Mrs. Mathew)

The fourth-born, Opeyemi, thrives in the fabric and textile industry. Married to Pastor Mathew, they raise a godly family, continuing the faith legacy.

Pastor Okewunmi Abayomi (Yomi)

The fifth-born, Yomi, is a scholar and minister of the Gospel. Senior Pastor at Salvation Proclaimers Anointed Church, founder of Edu Top Consultant, married to Dr. (Mrs.) Kehinde Okewunmi, blessed with a son.

Okewunmi Motunrayo (Mrs. Lucky Aba)

The sixth-born, Motunrayo, is a Laboratory Scientist, fashionista, NGO founder, minister of the Word, and entrepreneur. Married to Mr. Lucky Aba, blessed with a son.

Okewunmi Timileyin

The seventh-born, Timileyin, is a graduate of Counseling Psychology, poet, novelist, software developer, cybersecurity graduate, and media director. Unmarried but in a promising relationship.

Okewunmi Pelumi Paul

The last-born, Pelumi Paul, is a law student, tech entrepreneur, motivational speaker, Amazon affiliate marketer, and poet. Known as the youngest motivational speaker in Africa.
