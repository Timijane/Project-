Welcome to the Okewunmi Dynasty

The Okewunmi Dynasty is a family rooted in truth, sacrifice, faith, and legacy. At the heart of this great family is Elder Solomon Okewunmi, a man widely respected for his integrity, wisdom, and selfless devotion.

Born and raised in Ogun State, Egba, Abeokuta—Itori Oke, Elder Solomon has lived his life as a shining example of what it means to serve both family and community with unwavering commitment. By his side is his beloved wife, Deaconess Adebola Okewunmi, a virtuous woman and nurturing mother of many.
